How we work

Welcome to voidVoice, your confidential platform for addressing and resolving college-related issues. We understand that college life can sometimes present challenges that require a safe space for expression. Our platform empowers college students to voice their concerns anonymously, ensuring their privacy while enabling them to seek the assistance they need. Our team with the help of UGC will make sure that your problem gets resolved as fast as possible.

Anonymous Posting:

Once a student has signed up, the person can post his/her problem that they are facing inside the college. As the problems which would be posted are expected to be quite serious, so they need to submit their request along with a college official document which could make ourselves sure that it is an actual request, not a fake one.

Sharing the problem has two ways:

  • Only Authority: The problem can only be seen by the officials and take steps accordingly.
  • Chatbot: Posting a problem in the chatbot doesnâ€™t reveal any identity; only the problem is sent to the users around the globe. By this, the person could get suggestions from the globe and may not require higher authority help.

    • NOTE: The college document only needs to be shared only once, at the time of problem posting. Also, we would make sure that the document you share with us is a confidential one and no one else other than our officials would get access to it.

    Community Support:

    Once all verification of the problem along with document given is done. Our officials will keep your problems in front of UGC and start the process of solving it. UGC will verify the problem, and after inspection, the problem will get solved.

    On the other hand, the problems that are posted in the chatbot, the users from the globe would be suggesting a solution to the problem, alongside the officials.
